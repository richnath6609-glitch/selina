Who We Are

Committed to Preserving Cocoa Heritage

Heritage Cocoa Ltd. is a Ghana-based cocoa company dedicated to maintaining the rich cocoa heritage of West Africa.

We work closely with farmers to ensure high-quality production while improving livelihoods in cocoa-growing communities. We believe cocoa is more than a crop—it is a heritage that connects farmers, communities, and global chocolate lovers.

Our Mission

To provide high-quality cocoa while supporting farmers and promoting sustainable agriculture.

Our Vision

To become a leading cocoa supplier known for quality, integrity, and sustainability.

Our Core Operations Focus On:

  • Cocoa sourcing and aggregation
  • Quality control and processing
  • Export and international trade

Our Process

From Farm to Export

We maintain perfection at every stage of the cocoa journey.

1. Sourcing & Selection

We work directly with farmers in Twifo Praso to select only the finest, most robust cocoa pods for processing.

2. Scientific Fermentation

Our proprietary fermentation process ensures the specific flavor precursors are perfectly developed.

3. Natural Sun Drying

Beans are dried under the Ghanaian sun on raised platforms, ensuring optimal moisture and air circulation.

4. Quality Certification

Every batch undergoes rigorous lab testing to meet international standards for export-grade cocoa.

Empowering Our Farmers

We don't just buy cocoa; we invest in the people who grow it. We partner closely with local farmers, cooperatives, and strategic partners like COCOBOD to maintain the highest standards of cocoa cultivation.

Through comprehensive training and ongoing support, we help farmers improve their productivity, increase their yield quality, and ultimately improve their income and livelihoods.
